Process

FROM ZERO TO
GLOBAL IN 4.

01
Connect Your Repo
Link GitHub, GitLab, or Bitbucket. We auto-detect your framework and configure everything. No boilerplate.
$ joybit link github.com/you/app
02
Configure in Seconds
Set env variables, pick region preferences, define custom domains. Or don't — our defaults are production-ready.
$ joybit env set DATABASE_URL=...
03
Deploy to the Edge
Push your code. Watch it propagate to 38 edge nodes worldwide in under 90 seconds. Your custom domain is instant.
$ git push && joybit deploy
04
Scale Without Thinking
Our ML engine predicts load spikes before they hit. You sleep, we scale. Billed per millisecond of real usage.
$ joybit scale --auto --min=0 --max=∞
